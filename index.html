About

I am an animation artist working in Orlando as an intern to various firms. I worked at E2i Creative Studio as an animator as well as at the UCF Games Research Lab as a 3D artist. I graduated from UCF with a Bachelors in Fine Arts majoring in Experimental Animation. After graduation, I plans to evolve in my craft and continue his career as an animator.

Artist Statement

"My work focus on creating art from adversity, making more with less, making lemonades out of lemons. I find that working within limitation can be as fulfilling as working with complete creative freedom. I am interested in taking a bad situation and creating something new or better with it. Whether it be limited time, budget, skill, or scope, I will find a way to make it interesting."
