Aphasia VR Project

The Aphasia Virtual Reality Project is designed to help patients with aphasia, the lose of speech due to trauma, by encouraging communication and interaction with the characters and environments of the virtual world.

PTSD VR Project

This is a showcase of the PTSD virtual reality project for E2i Creative Studio. This project is designed to help patients with PTSD cope with their trauma and help therapist identify their patients trigger. The goal is to put the patients in a safe, normal environment with stimuli that could help identify their triggers.
